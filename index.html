Change Management: The Switch Framework

Ensuring sustainable transformation with your experienced team

We employ the Switch Framework from Chip and Dan Heath to address the emotional side (Elephant), rational side (Rider), and environment (Path) for lasting change.

Direct the Rider

Clear Direction

  • • Identify and amplify what's already working
  • • Define specific, actionable steps
  • • Paint compelling picture of serving 25% more agencies

Motivate the Elephant

Engage Hearts

  • • Connect changes to mission impact
  • • Start with achievable quick wins
  • • Frame as professional development, not replacement

Shape the Path

Make it Easy

  • • Modify systems so right behaviors become automatic
  • • Create templates and checklists
  • • Leverage peer influence through change champions

This approach respects your team's expertise while clearing the path for greater impact.
